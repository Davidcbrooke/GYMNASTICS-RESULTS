The Journey
The NGL Judging Pathway
Four levels. Every one earned by doing the job — or fast-tracked with a British Gymnastics qualification.
🎓
1. Trainee Judge
Unlocks: Shadowing
  • Everyone starts here
  • No qualifications needed
🌱
2. Novice Judge
Unlocks: Second Judge
  • NGL training course
  • Quiz score 70%+
  • Shadowed 2+ competitions
or: BG Club Judge + training + 80%+ quiz
🥈
3. Accredited Judge
Unlocks: Full Judge
  • Quiz score 90%+
  • Second-judged 5+ competitions
  • NGL training course
or: BG Regional Judge + training
🏆
4. Expert Judge
The top of the pathway
  • All Accredited criteria
  • Judged 5+ comps on one piece
or: BG National/Brevet + training
